Devi Sadhanashwariji, Born on 16 Oct, 91 (9th day of Navratri) in Amravati City, Maharashtra (India).

It is said that upon noticing the dazzling glow on Her face, an Astrologer had predicted that this child would shine like a star and spread the ancient knowledge of Puranas all over Humanity.
Deviji has delivered spiritual discourses on Shrimad Bhagwat Katha, Srimad Devi Bhagavatam, Ram Katha and Shiv Maha Puran in almost all cities of Maharashtra Specially in Marathi & Hindi languages, with a unique way by relating it to an ordinary man’s day-to-day life.
Thousands of lives have been experienced peace, harmony and joy through the knowledge of Her oratory skills and sweet singing voice.
